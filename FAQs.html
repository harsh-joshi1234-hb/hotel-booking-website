Frequently Asked Questions

What are the check-in and check-out times?

The standard check-in time is 2:00 PM IST, and check-out is 12:00 PM IST. Early check-in or late check-out may be available upon request and is subject to availability.

Is parking available at the hotels?

Yes, most hotels listed on our platform offer on-site parking for guests, which is often complimentary. Please check the specific hotel's amenities list for confirmation.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellation policies vary by hotel and the rate type you've booked. You can find the detailed cancellation policy for your specific booking in your confirmation email and on the hotel's page before confirming your reservation.

Do you offer airport shuttle services?

Some hotels provide airport shuttle services, either as a complimentary service or for an additional fee. This information is available in the hotel details section on the booking page.
